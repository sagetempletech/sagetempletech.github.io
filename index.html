About Me

Temple Iyoha

I'm a passionate full-stack developer and UI/UX expert with over 8 years of experience in creating digital solutions that solve real-world problems. Based in Benin City, Nigeria, I've worked with businesses of all sizes, from local startups to international corporations.

My journey in tech began at the University of Benin, where I earned my degree in Computer Science. Since then, I've continuously expanded my skills through hands-on experience, professional certifications, and staying updated with the latest industry trends.

I believe in creating technology that is not only functional but also beautiful and user-friendly. My approach combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of user needs and business goals to deliver solutions that drive real results.
